ABOUT US

We are littlepaws

The littlepaws Pet Foundation is a national animal welfare organization dedicated to ending pet homelessness.

For almost one decade, littlepaws PF. has helped in creating true social change by bringing pet adoption into the mainstream. Our work has helped make a difference to the Vietnamese rescue community and thousands of pets in need of rescue and rehabilitation.

But, until every pet is safe, respected, and loved, we all still have big, hairy work to do. Find out more about our mission to help save over 10,000 healthy and rehomable pets each year.

FIND OUT MORE ABOUT US

9 years of impact,
88,888 lives touched and counting!

littlepaws PF. supports programs and initiatives focused on addressing the pet overpopulation crisis in all 63 provinces/cities. Through donation and fundrasing for pet adoption, spay & neuter, microchipping and emergencies we are making a difference to reduce the number of pets in shelters and rescues.

and product donations

1.2 billion Vietnamese Dong in food and product donations

Thanks to our major partner - MARS Petcare, littlepaws PF. national food donation program delivers millions of dongs worth of food for free to vulnerable and at-risk pets.

Thousands of tonnes of food are generously donated by MARS Petcare, and together with our partners, littlepaws PF. manages the logistics of this massive operation - ensuring these truckloads of pet food are delivered to the right place at the right time for hundreds of rescue groups, shelters, and pounds across Vietnam.

OUR PROGRAMS

We littlepaws is here to help with our best staffs

Every day, shelters and rescues across Vietnam work tirelessly to address the pet overpopulation crisis. littlepaws PF. is here to help. We offer a variety of programs and grant assistance to help animal welfare organizations change the outlook for the pets of their communities.

Sitting

littlepaws Pet Foundation's facility in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam provides shelters and fosters for many of our dogs and cats. In addition to a roof over their heads, we pay for heat, food, water, electricity, and general maintenance.

Grooming

With proper dogs and cats grooming, you will get rid of shedding, fleas, ticks, and various health conditions. It will also allow you to identify any underlying conditions or diseases early. Therefore, you will be able to take your pets for treatment sooner and more efficiently.

Vetting
