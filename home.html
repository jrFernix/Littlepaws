ABOUT US

We are littlepaws

littlepaws Pet Foundation is a national animal welfare organization dedicated to ending pet homelessness.

For almost one decade, littlepaws PF. has helped in creating true social change by bringing pet adoption into the mainstream. Our work has helped make a difference to the Vietnamese rescue community and thousands of pets in need of rescue and rehabilitation. But, until every pet is safe, respected, and loved, we all still have big, hairy work to do. Find out more about our mission to help save over 10,000 healthy and rehomable pets each year.

OUR PROGRAMS

We littlepaws is here to help with our best staffs

Every day, shelters and rescues across Vietnam work tirelessly to address the pet overpopulation crisis. littlepaws PF. is here to help. We offer a variety of programs and grant assistance to help animal welfare organizations change the outlook for the pets of their communities.

Img

Sitting

littlepaws Pet Foundation's facility in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam provides shelters and fosters for many of our dogs and cats. In addition to a roof over their heads, we pay for heat, food, water, electricity, and general maintenance.

While most of the work is done by dedicated volunteers, we have a small kennel staff to help care for the pets.

Read more

Grooming

It is an important aspect of a pet’s overall well-being and health. Pet grooming is important for the whole family as well.

With proper dogs and cats grooming, you will get rid of shedding, fleas, ticks, and various health conditions. It will also allow you to identify any underlying conditions or diseases early. Therefore, you will be able to take your pets for treatment sooner and more efficiently.

Read more

Vetting

Our clinic provides vet care to our dogs and cats. General health assessments, medications, vaccinations, blood work, testing and other necessary surgeries are all a part of the day- to-day routine.

Additionally, we pay for specialist care, as needed, to maintain the health of our pets.

Read more

Spay & Neuter

Every year, millions of unwanted dogs and cats are euthanized. By having your dog or cat sterilized, you will do your part to prevent the birth of unwanted puppies and kittens.

Spaying and neutering prevent unwanted litters, help protect against some serious health problems, and may reduce many of the behavioral problems associated with the mating instinct.

Read more

Training

Training your pets - whether you just got a puppy/kitten or want to teach an old pet new tricks - can be a rewarding experience for both you and your pet.

Training the pets not only keeps them safer in unpredictable situations, it’s also an excellent way to strengthen your bond and deepen your relationship.

Read more

Cremation

We believe your treasured pets are important part of the family and should be honoured when they pass away. We offer affordable pet cremation services that are suitable for dogs, cats at our on-site crematorium.

All pets are handled with care and compassion and we guarantee all of your pet’s ashes are returned in either a scatter box or pet urn.

Read more

ASSISTED REHOMING

Do you want we littlepaws to rehome your beloved pet?

There are many different situations that can lead to you needing to rehome your beloved pet.

Our program AssistedRehoming can help you find the best new home for your pet while they stay safe and stress-free in your home, but if we can, we’d love to help you and your pet stay together.

Below, you’ll find some great advice and resources. These have been gathered based on the most common enquiries received by the littlepaws PF. help desk to help you make the best decision for your pet.
