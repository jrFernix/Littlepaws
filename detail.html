Medical notes

She is a brachycephalic breed that is always at risk of boas ( brachycephalic obstructive airway syndrome). We expect you to have a complete understanding of the condition and discuss the potential surgical, environmental and medical modifications you may have to do for this condition with your vet.

She is very healthy and has passed all of his vet checks. She has not had airway surgery as she breathes very well but this is something that may need to be done in the future.
