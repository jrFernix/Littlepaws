WHO WE ARE

The littlepaws Pet Foundation is a national animal welfare charity with a vision of a future where every pet is safe, respected and loved.

OUR STORY

Overview of littlepaws

Tragically, thousands of homeless dogs and cats are needlessly euthanized in Vietnam each year simply because of pet overpopulation. littlepaws Pet Foundation is an organization that provides a second chance for thousands of homeless dogs and cat through rescue programs, rehabilitation and transport to forever homes in communities throughout Vietnam that have a demand for adoptable pets.

Since starting operations in late 2010, littlepaws PF. has saved the lives of nearly 90,000 homeless companion animals by rescuing them from area shelters and transporting them to their new forever homes via our reputable rescue partners across Vietnam.

After working with various traditional rescue groups for many years, the founders of littlepaws PF. realized that the best way to save these dogs and cats is to get them to where there was actually a demand for homeless animals. By partnering with other reputable rescue groups, we knew we could save thousands of more pets than by adopting them out locally. In September 2013, littlepaws PF. began operations by transporting at-risk pets from everywhere to carefully vetted rescue partners in Ho Chi Minh City, our first destination City.

One thing that is unique about littlepaws PF.'s efforts is our “temporary” foster program. After we rescue pets from area shelters, the dogs and cats need short-term foster homes (a matter of days or up to a few weeks in some cases) until their transport date. We are constantly in need of fosters and donations to cover their extraordinary medical costs to make sure every pet transported is healthy.
